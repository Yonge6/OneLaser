Operating Principles
管理原则

所有资产都围绕产品、渠道、版本和使用状态管理，避免“找不到、拿错版、重复做、不能复用”。

中文

OneLaser 品牌资产管理目标

建立一个以产品系列为核心的品牌资产管理 SOP，覆盖 Brand、Cobra、Hydra Gen1、Hydra Gen2、XRF、VertiGo、New Product、AllSeries、Accessories 等一级目录。每一份资产必须能回答四个问题：属于哪个产品或品牌层级、服务哪个渠道、当前是否可发布、谁负责维护。

English

OneLaser brand asset objective

Build a product-first SOP for managing OneLaser brand assets across Brand, Cobra, Hydra Gen1, Hydra Gen2, XRF, VertiGo, New Product, AllSeries, and Accessories. Every asset must clearly show its product owner, channel use, release status, and maintenance owner.

SOP Workflow
标准流程

从需求创建到归档复盘，所有渠道都走同一套生命周期；差异只体现在交付规格和审批人。

Brief / 需求

确认产品、渠道、目标受众、交付规格、截止时间和负责人。No asset work starts without a product, channel, format, due date, and owner.

Source / 素材

收集官方图、客户图、3D、文案、参数和合规声明。Use approved source material before generating derivative assets.

Create / 制作

按渠道模板制作，并保留源文件。Keep editable source files for web, ads, brochure, social, and sales collateral.

Review / 审核

品牌、产品、市场、销售按风险审核。Brand, product, marketing, and sales approve based on asset risk.

Release / 发布

仅发布 Approved 文件夹中的资产。Only assets in Approved are allowed for public or client-facing use.

Distribute / 分发

按渠道同步到官网、Amazon、广告账户、销售资料库或社媒排期。Distribute to the correct channel repository.

Measure / 复盘

记录使用渠道、投放表现、客户反馈和复用机会。Capture performance, feedback, and reuse signals.

Archive / 归档

过期、替换、暂停使用的资产进入 Archive，禁止继续外发。Retired assets are archived and marked unavailable.

资产目录规范

以下目录按照产品线一级目录版 SOP 展开：先按产品线归档，再在每个产品线内部按渠道、物料类型和制作阶段归档。

Brand

母品牌资产，不归属于单一产品。

VIS / 视觉识别系统

包含 LOGO、Brand Typefaces 品牌字体、VIS Manual。Logo 文件必须保留横版、竖版、单色、反白、图标版；字体需标明授权范围；VIS Manual 作为所有渠道视觉判断依据。

  • English: Store logo masters, brand typefaces, and the VIS manual. Include usage rules, clear space, color values, and prohibited applications.
  • Owner: Brand Design. Approval: Marketing Lead.
Engraved Finished Product Photos / 雕刻成品照片

分为 Official Releases 官方出品、Client Images 客户图片、AIGC。客户图片必须记录授权状态；AIGC 必须标注生成日期、提示词用途和不得用于参数证明。

  • English: Separate official releases, client images, and AI-generated images. Client images require usage permission. AI images cannot be used as performance proof.
  • Status labels: Raw, Retouched, Approved, Restricted.
Brand Merchandise Design / 品牌周边设计

用于展会、客户礼品、内部活动和渠道伙伴包。必须保留效果图、打样图、供应商文件和最终生产文件。

  • English: Manage merchandise mockups, samples, vendor-ready files, and final production files.
  • Required: product mockup, source file, print spec, vendor contact, production date.
Brand Compliance / 品牌合规

任何修改 Logo、品牌色、字体、品牌口号、品牌承诺的资产都必须进入 Brand Review。未经审批不得在官网、广告、展会或销售材料中使用。

  • English: Any asset that changes logo, color, typography, slogan, or brand claims requires brand review before release.

Cobra

SOP 中最完整的产品目录模板，其它产品线按此复制。

GTM / 上市策略

保存产品定位、目标人群、核心卖点、竞品对比、渠道计划、上市时间表和发布复盘。GTM 是所有后续资产的源头。

  • English: Store positioning, audience, value proposition, competitor comparison, channel plan, launch timeline, and post-launch review.
  • Deliverables: GTM brief, messaging house, launch calendar, launch retrospective.
SEM / 搜索广告

包含关键词、广告文案、落地页版本、素材尺寸、UTM、投放截图和表现复盘。与网页设计目录中的 Live Page 保持链接。

  • English: Manage keywords, ad copy, landing pages, creative sizes, UTM rules, campaign screenshots, and performance notes.
Social / 社媒内容

渠道包括 TikTok、Instagram、YouTube；KOL 子目录保存 Influencer & Creator Content 网红和创作者内容。所有外部创作者内容必须附授权范围。

  • English: Organize platform-ready content for TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, and KOL collaborations. Influencer content requires rights, term, territory, and usage channel.
  • Required folders: Scripts, Raw Footage, Edited, Captions, Thumbnails, Approved.
Sales Collateral / 销售资料

包含 Merchandise Design 周边产品设计、Ads 广告、Email 邮件。Ads 下按 Meta 和 Google 分开，Meta 覆盖 Facebook and Instagram paid ads，Google 覆盖 Google Ads、YouTube、Display。

  • English: Separate merchandise, ads, and email assets. Split paid ads by Meta and Google to prevent platform spec confusion.
  • Sales-use files must be exported as PDF, PNG/JPG, and editable source where relevant.
Campaigns / 活动

保存促销、节日、展会、渠道活动、联名活动和新品预热。每个 Campaign 目录必须包含 Brief、Creative、Media Plan、Approved、Report。

  • English: Use campaign folders for promotion, holiday, event, partner, and teaser work. Every campaign needs a brief, creative assets, media plan, approved exports, and report.
Product / 产品资产

这是 Cobra 的核心目录。包含 Brochure 宣传册、Video 视频、3D Renders 3D 渲染图、3D Model，以及 Web Design 网页设计、Amazon Storefront Design 亚马逊店铺设计。

  • English: The product folder is the source of truth for brochure, video, 3D renders, 3D models, web design, and Amazon storefront assets.
  • Examples from SOP: ONELASER_Cobra_Brochure_EN_2026Q2_v2, ONELASER_Cobra10_3DModel_EN_2026Q2_v1.
Web Design / 网页设计

包含 Figma Link、Teaser Page、Live Page。Live Page 下保存 Banner、Hero & Secondary Images 主副图、Cropped Assets 切图、Mobile 手机端、Desktop 电脑端、Source Files 源文件。

  • English: Web design must keep Figma links, teaser pages, live pages, banners, hero and secondary images, cropped assets, mobile, desktop, and source files.
  • Source file types: PSD, AI, Figma, video project, 3D project, copy doc.
Amazon Storefront / 亚马逊店铺

保存店铺模块图、A+ 内容、主图、详情图、尺寸导出、合规文本和上架截图。与 Product 和 Sales Collateral 共享素材，但最终导出独立存档。

  • English: Storefront assets include module images, A+ content, listing images, exported sizes, compliance copy, and live screenshots.

Hydra Gen1

旧产品或上一代产品资产。

Lifecycle / 生命周期

Hydra Gen1 应标注当前销售状态：Active、Limited Support、Discontinued。所有新制作资产必须注明是否继续对外使用。

  • English: Mark lifecycle status clearly so old product material is not reused as current product content by mistake.
Migration / 迁移

若 Hydra Gen2 已替代同一卖点，Gen1 资产需要在标题或说明中加入 Replacement Note，指向 Gen2 对应资产。

  • English: Add replacement notes that point from Gen1 assets to the current Gen2 equivalents when applicable.

Hydra Gen2

当前产品线资产。

Product Template / 产品模板

Hydra Gen2 使用 Cobra 的完整目录模板：GTM、SEM、Social、Sales Collateral、Campaigns、Product、Web Design、Amazon Storefront。

  • English: Apply the Cobra template to maintain consistent launch and channel asset operations.
Version Priority / 版本优先级

如 Gen2 与 Gen1 存在同名素材，默认以 Gen2 为公开使用版本。Gen1 仅用于历史、售后或特定经销商沟通。

  • English: Gen2 is the default public version when Gen1 and Gen2 have similar assets.

XRF

产品资产与销售渠道并重。

Required Set / 必备资产包

XRF 至少需要 Brochure、Product Photos、Video、Web Hero、Sales Deck、FAQ、Ads、Email、Amazon Storefront。涉及参数和结果证明的内容必须经产品负责人确认。

  • English: Claims, parameters, and proof images require product-owner approval before release.
Claim Control / 卖点管控

性能、精度、材料兼容性、速度等表达统一放入 Messaging House，广告和销售材料不得自行改写核心声明。

  • English: Performance claims must come from the approved messaging house.

VertiGo

产品内容和渠道资料。

Channel Pack / 渠道包

VertiGo 目录需提供渠道伙伴可直接使用的图片、短视频、单页、FAQ、参数表、邮件模板和社媒文案。

  • English: Provide ready-to-use partner packs with images, short video, one-pager, FAQ, spec sheet, email templates, and social copy.
Localization / 本地化

多语言资产按照语言码分开，如 EN、DE、FR、ES、ZH。不得把翻译中的版本放入 Approved。

  • English: Keep localization files by language code and only release reviewed translations.

New Product

新品预发布暂存区。

Confidential Staging / 保密暂存

所有未正式命名或未发布新品先进入 New Product。文件夹名称使用项目代号，不在公开资产中暴露最终命名、价格、发布日期或技术参数。

  • English: Use project codenames before official launch. Do not expose final naming, pricing, launch date, or specs in public-facing exports.
Graduation / 转正

新品完成品牌命名、产品定位、首批资产和上市审批后，从 New Product 迁移为独立一级目录或并入现有产品线。

  • English: Move a new product into its own primary folder only after naming, positioning, first asset set, and launch approval are complete.

AllSeries

跨产品系列资产。

Comparison Assets / 对比资料

保存产品矩阵、系列对比、购买指南、应用场景汇总、官网集合页和全系列宣传册。参数来自各产品负责人确认后的最新版。

  • English: Use this folder for product comparison, buying guides, application overviews, collection pages, and all-series brochures.
Shared Claims / 共用卖点

如使用“全系列适用”的声明，必须确认每个产品型号都符合，不允许用单一产品表现代表全系列。

  • English: All-series claims must be true for every included model.

Accessories

配件、套装和附加产品。

Accessory Mapping / 配件映射

每个配件需标注适配产品、SKU、图片、应用场景、安装说明、兼容限制和上架素材。与主产品共享图片时必须保留引用路径。

  • English: Every accessory needs compatible products, SKU, images, use cases, install notes, compatibility limits, and listing assets.
Bundle Assets / 套装资产

套装图必须显示包含物、适配关系和购买价值。不同市场价格或配置不同，按区域建立子目录。

  • English: Bundle assets must show included items, compatibility, and buying value. Split by region when configuration differs.

Release Checklist
发布检查表

任何资产进入 Approved 或 Published 前，至少完成以下检查。

Brand / 品牌

Logo、颜色、字体、版式、品牌语气符合 VIS Manual。No distorted logo, wrong color, unlicensed font, or off-brand tone.

Product / 产品

型号、参数、卖点、兼容性、价格和使用场景已由产品负责人确认。Claims are accurate and approved.

Channel / 渠道

尺寸、比例、文件格式、文案长度、平台限制符合发布渠道要求。Exports match platform specs.

Rights / 权利

客户图片、KOL 内容、音乐、字体、AIGC、第三方素材均记录授权范围。Usage rights are traceable.

Files / 文件

源文件、导出文件、预览图、文案和链接完整，命名符合规则。Source and export files are complete.

Version / 版本

旧版本已移入 Archive 或标注 Superseded，不再对外分发。Old versions are retired or marked clearly.

SOP 不是一次性整理，而是每次发布、每周、每月、每季都要轻量维护。

Weekly

Monthly
